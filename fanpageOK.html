Biography

"The Backstreet Boys" is an American band formed in 1993 in Orlando, Florida. Its members are:

Name (best known as) Birthdate Place of Birth
1.- Kevin Richardson October 3, 1971 Lexington, Kentucky
2.- Howie Dorough August 22, 1973 Orlando, Florida
3.- Brian Littrell February 20, 1975 Lexington, Kentucky
4.- AJ McLean January 9, 1978 West Palm Beach, Florida
5.- Nick Carter January 28, 1980 Jamestown, New York

They first became internationally famous with their album "Backstreet Boys" released in 1996. Their success kept growing with their following albums and they became the most popular boy band in the world in the late 90's and early 2000's.

Over two decades have passed since their musical career started, and the Backstreet Boys keep renovating themselves and continue to be one of the most beloved boy bands in modern music history.
